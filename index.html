About us

Our ambition is to keep children’s natural curiosity and spontaneity as the center of all programs and activities.Our unique approach to Pre-school and Elementary education eliminates bags, homework, and exams, focusing instead on activity-based learning. We offer a range of exciting activities including Taekwondo, swimming, foreign languages, and various sports. Our holistic programs like Artes Summer Camp and Artes Kids Club, along with our emphasis on creativity, personal health, and community involvement, make Artes a vibrant and nurturing environment for every child.


Classroom

M&M is the best Day care Center and School in Karachi that offers fun-filled learning for toddlers to allow greater learning for children. With activity-based learning we work to enhance the child’s skill to earn, understand, adapt, and rationalize at an early age.Subject being taught are Mathematics, English Language, Urdu Language, Chinese Language, Personal Health Development, Ethics and Speech & Communication.
